About

As far as his homesteading wife is concerned, Bletcher bathes in the mundane juices of his mundane corporate job salarayman sweat. He has a mundane suit and a mundane patchy beard, a mundane wage and a mundane day-to-day routine; and that's all she's ever asked for (being the runaway half-blood princess of the Demon Lord and all)

Unbeknownst to her, Bletcher is a serial adventerer of faraway planes, risking life and limb against all odds with nothing but his briefcase and stale corporate decorum. All to return home with a new magical gift each day for his lovely wife.

The serialised comic adventures of fantasy, sci-fi, romance, and common homely inconveniences, written by Fletcher Fraher and illustrated by Victoria "Biggus" Bramham for the decentralised web, using the Rarebit web-comic framework.
